# Branding Guardrails

- Keep all existing routes, section anchors, and form flows working.
- Use the supplied KoreField logo as the primary brand mark where space allows.
- Blend the UI toward a black, white, and neon-green industrial palette without sacrificing readability.
- Keep clear links between the official site and the Academy subdomain in both directions.
- Preserve the mobile hamburger menu and keep navigation compact on small screens.
- Keep button sizes restrained and avoid motion that feels flashy or distracting.
- Do not remove content or restructure sections unless required for brand consistency.
- Prefer centered, balanced card layouts on mobile and desktop.
