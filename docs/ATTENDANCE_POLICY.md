# Attendance Policy — KoreField Academy

## Core Principle

KoreField Academy does NOT use mandatory attendance as a progression or certification requirement. Certification is strictly deliverables-based and performance-gated.

## What Matters (Deliverables-Based)

Learner advancement and certification depend entirely on:

1. **Performance Gate scores** — must meet threshold at each module/level boundary
2. **Assessment submissions** — code exercises, quizzes, lab work must be submitted and graded
3. **Pod deliverables** — working prototype, documentation, governance checklist, sprint reviews, presentation
4. **Capstone defense** — project submitted and defense passed with panel of 2+ assessors
5. **Assessor approval** — formal certification eligibility approval
6. **Payment clearance** — no outstanding balances

## What Does NOT Matter

- Number of live lab sessions attended
- Login frequency (though this feeds dropout risk scoring)
- Time spent on platform
- Number of video lessons watched

## Lab Session Flexibility

- Lab sessions are scheduled but NOT mandatory
- If a learner misses a scheduled lab session:
  - Session recording available within 24 hours
  - Learner can submit lab work asynchronously
  - No penalty for missing the live session
- Learners are notified 48 hours in advance of scheduled sessions

## Engagement Monitoring (Not Attendance Tracking)

The platform monitors engagement signals for dropout risk detection, NOT for attendance enforcement:
- Login frequency
- Submission timeliness
- Assessment scores
- Pod participation

These signals feed the Dropout Risk Agent, which triggers intervention workflows (assessor notification + re-engagement recommendations) when risk thresholds are breached. This is supportive, not punitive.

## Pause and Resume

- Learners can voluntarily pause their learning (payment pause)
- Progress is preserved during pause
- Access restored on payment resume
- No penalty for pausing — schedule recalculated on resume
