# KoreField Academy

KoreField Academy is an applied AI learning platform designed to build Africa's workforce for the future of intelligent industries. The platform delivers globally credible, performance-gated AI education through AI avatar-led teaching, instructor-led labs, pod-based collaboration, assessor-guided supervision, and capstone defense.

## Platform Vision

KoreField Academy exists to close the AI skills gap across Africa and emerging markets by providing structured, rigorous, and accessible AI education that produces job-ready professionals. The platform combines the best of human instruction with AI-augmented learning to deliver outcomes that are verifiable, traceable, and industry-aligned.

## Target Market

- Primary: African professionals and graduates seeking AI, data science, cybersecurity, and product leadership skills
- Secondary: Global learners in emerging markets seeking affordable, high-quality AI education
- Corporate: Organisations sponsoring cohorts of learners for workforce development

## Two-Layer Curriculum

### Layer 1 — Foundation School (Free)
A mandatory, free intellectual base that all learners must complete before accessing paid content. Covers AI literacy, prompt engineering awareness, systems awareness, governance, and professional discipline across 5 modules.

### Layer 2 — Track Pathways (Paid)
Structured learning pathways with Beginner → Intermediate → Advanced levels. Learners enroll in a full pathway, not individual levels.

**Four Launch Tracks:**
1. AI Engineering and Intelligent Systems
2. Data Science and Decision Intelligence
3. Cybersecurity and AI Security
4. AI Product and Project Leadership

## Key Differentiators

- Performance-gated progression (no attendance-only certificates)
- Pod-based collaboration simulating real delivery teams
- AI Avatar-led instruction powered by RAG-based agents
- Assessor supervision ensuring industry realism
- Region-aware pricing with purchasing power parity
- Verifiable, traceable certificates gated by composite eligibility checks
- Market intelligence AI agents for strategic decision-making
