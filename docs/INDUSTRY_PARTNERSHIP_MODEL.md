# Industry Partnership Model — KoreField Academy

## Overview

KoreField Academy partners with organisations (Corporate Partners) who sponsor learners, monitor cohort performance, manage billing, and access a hiring pipeline of certified graduates.

## Partnership Types

### 1. Cohort Sponsorship
- Organisation sponsors a group of learners for specific tracks
- Sponsorship covers full or partial tuition (via Scholarship/SCH-* records)
- Organisation selects tracks and number of sponsored seats
- Sponsored learners follow the same curriculum, gates, and certification requirements as self-paying learners

### 2. Hiring Pipeline Access
- Corporate Partners gain visibility into certified graduates from their sponsored cohorts
- Access to: track specializations, capstone results, assessor recommendations, professionalism scores
- Enables direct hiring from the Academy's certified talent pool

### 3. Custom Cohort Reporting
- Cohort performance summaries: average scores, completion rates, certification rates
- Comparison against platform-wide benchmarks
- Regular reporting cadence (configurable per partner)

## Corporate Partner Onboarding

1. Partnership agreement signed (offline)
2. Admin creates CorporatePartner account with organisation details
3. Corporate Partner receives login credentials (MFA required)
4. Admin assigns sponsored learner seats to the partner
5. Sponsored learners enroll via standard flow with sponsorship applied at checkout

## Financial Model

- Sponsorship amount recorded as Scholarship (SCH-*) adjustment
- Deducted from learner's final payable amount by Pricing Intelligence Engine
- Corporate Partner billed separately (invoices via Corporate Portal)
- Payment reconciliation managed by Finance Admin

## Data Boundaries

- Corporate Partners can ONLY see their own sponsored learners
- No access to other partners' data or platform-wide metrics
- No ability to modify learner records, grades, or certifications
- Read-only access to performance data

## Future Expansion

- Custom track requests from corporate partners
- Dedicated cohort scheduling
- White-label certificate branding
- API integration for partner HR systems
